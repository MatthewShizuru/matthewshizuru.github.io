2Day App

Fall 2016

Project Explanation

For this project we entered a contest and had to create an app for a major problem in school today. The first step to completing this project was brainstorming what my group found to be problematic in school. We decided we wanted to take a couple problems that we all struggled with and combine them into an app that can make a students life easier. We had to go through the design and layout of our app and also the content that we wanted in our app.

Project Reflection

During this project my group faced many problems and had to overcome the problems we encountered. Almost every part of this project was difficult especially when creating the app. My group had a tough time coming up with an app idea because we wanted to make sure we had the best idea. Another problem we ran into was how we would code the app. Several other groups in our class all used a different software to code their app. We were left with the choice of what we wanted to use and eventually decided on just coding the app through HTML. Overall the difficulties faced in this project helped use learn about other softwares used to code apps and problems that students face today.


Life on the Mississippi Essay

Winter 2016

Project Explanation

In my English 11 Honors class we were to read a passage from the book Life on the Mississippi and analyze how language can affect how things are perceived. We then had to write an essay on the language used in the passage and explain why Mark Twain used the language he did.

Project Reflection

I found this essay to be challenging for many reasons. The main reason was because the passage was a little hard to understand. I wasn’t familiar with some of the words and phrases that Twain used to describe his experience as a student. However, once I was able to decipher the words I hadn’t understood, it was easy to understand why Twain had used the words he did. Each word and phrase has a connotation whether it’s positive, negative, or neutral. By carefully choosing words for their connotative meaning you are able to create a more meaningful writing piece.


Aesthete Presentation

Fall/Winter 2016

Project Explanation

In my graphic design class we were given the task of making a company and establishing our company's purpose. Our teacher put us in groups of 3-4 and we had to collaborate with our company members to create a new company. Our company had to be within the graphic design field, so we came up with the name Aesthete. Aesthete comes from the word aesthetic, and aesthete means an appreciation for art. We felt this fit our company’s purpose and also our image that we wanted to portray. This slide show showcased all of our group members logos for our company and also a brief explanation of what our company was about.

Project Reflection

I found this project to be a good learning experience. With my program area dealing with Web Design, graphics and art are a big part of web design. Collaborating with my classmates brought to my attention that we all had different ways of designing. Having each other explain why we made each design was interesting to listen to how they designed it. I was able to get a better understanding of how our minds worked differently, but we could also put our ideas together. Overall, I found this project to be very helpful in my understanding of working with others.
